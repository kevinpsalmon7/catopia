Catopia — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026. Operated by Kévin Psalmon ("we", "us"). Contact: contact@catopia.app.

This policy explains what data Catopia ("the app") collects, how we use it, and your choices, including how to delete your data.

1. Data we collect

  • Account data. When you sign up (email/password, Sign in with Apple, or Sign in with Google), our authentication provider Clerk processes your email address and a user identifier.
  • Gameplay data (metadata only). When you catch a cat, we store the keepsake metadata (a generated name, breed guess, rarity, timestamp, and the coarse location) in our backend Convex, so features like the map and leaderboards work.
  • Location. With your permission, we tag captures with your location. You can revoke this in iOS Settings.
  • Photos stay on your device. Photos are processed on-device and never uploaded. Only the metadata above leaves the device.
  • Diagnostics and analytics. With your consent, we collect anonymous crash reports and basic usage analytics.
  • Purchases. Subscriptions and in-app purchases are processed by Apple. We receive purchase status, not your payment details.
  • Advertising. The free version may show ads. Where required, we ask permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt before any tracking. Removing ads is a subscription benefit.

2. How we use data

To provide and secure your account, run gameplay features, process purchases, prevent abuse, fix crashes, and comply with the law. We do not sell your personal data.

3. Sharing

We share data only with service providers running the app on our behalf: Clerk (auth), Convex (backend), Apple (purchases, sign-in), our crash/analytics provider, and, in the free tier, advertising partners.

4. Retention and deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time in the app: Profile → Settings → Delete account. You may also email contact@catopia.app.

5. Your rights

Depending on your region (e.g. GDPR / CCPA), you may request access, correction, deletion, or portability, and withdraw consent. Contact contact@catopia.app.

6. Children

Catopia is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect their data.

7. Changes

We may update this policy; the "Last updated" date reflects the latest version.

Not legal advice. Have it reviewed for your jurisdiction before submitting to the App Store.
